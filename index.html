Studdy Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Studdy is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Studdy mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

When using Studdy, certain information may be collected in order to provide the functionality of the application.

Account Information

When creating an account, users may provide information such as an email address or username. This information is used solely for authentication and account management.

Study Data

Studdy allows users to store study-related information such as subjects, grades, study sessions, streaks, and academic progress. This data is stored to allow users to track their learning progress and improve their study habits.

AI Interactions

Studdy includes AI-powered features designed to assist with studying. Messages submitted to the AI assistant may be processed by third-party AI providers in order to generate responses.

Usage Data

Anonymous usage data may be collected to understand how the application is used, identify technical issues, and improve the overall experience.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain the functionality of the Studdy application
  • Allow users to track study sessions, grades, and academic progress
  • Enable AI-powered study assistance
  • Improve the performance and usability of the app
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Studdy takes reasonable measures to protect user data. Information is stored securely and access is limited to what is necessary for providing the app's functionality.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Studdy may rely on third-party services in order to provide certain features, including:

  • Artificial intelligence services for study assistance
  • Apple In-App Purchases for subscriptions
  • Hosting and infrastructure services

These third-party providers may process limited data only to perform the services required for the app.

5. User Control

Users may edit or delete their study data within the application. Users may also request deletion of their account and associated data.

6. Children's Privacy

Studdy is designed for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with the updated date.
